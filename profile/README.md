# Healdan Project

## "Healdan" - meaning

**Healdan** is an **Old English** verb meaning *“to hold, keep, preserve, guard, observe, maintain, or restrain.”*

### Common senses of healdan in Old English:
- To hold or grasp (physically)
- To keep or preserve (as in keeping a promise or keeping something safe)
- To rule or govern (holding authority)
- To observe rules or customs
- To restrain or control

## About the Healdan Project
**Healdan** builds on the power of **Alfresco Content Services Community Edition** and extends it into a truly project- and matter-centric platform. It delivers structured workspaces, enriched metadata, and streamlined collaboration features similar to those found in well-known commercial legal DMS solutions — without the complexity or licensing overhead. **Healdan** brings modern, secure, and intuitive document management to teams that need organized, governed content at the center of their work.
